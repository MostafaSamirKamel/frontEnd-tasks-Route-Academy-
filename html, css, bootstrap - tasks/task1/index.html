SPORTS CLUB

This is our featured activities, we hope you enjoy your time with us!

Football

Football, also known as soccer in some countries, is the world’s most popular sport. Played between two teams of eleven players, it involves using feet to maneuver a ball with the goal of scoring by getting it into the opponent’s net. 		Basketball

Basketball is a fast-paced team sport played by two teams of five players each. The objective is to score points by shooting a ball through the opponent’s hoop. It requires agility, coordination, and quick decision-making.
Volleyball

Volleyball is a dynamic sport where two teams of six players are separated by a net and aim to ground the ball on the opponent’s side. It emphasizes coordination, communication, and strategic play. 		Gym

Going to the gym involves using equipment and facilities to improve physical health. It supports strength training, cardio, and flexibility workouts, enhancing muscle tone and mental well-being.
Karate

Karate is a traditional Japanese martial art focused on self-defense, discipline, and physical conditioning. It involves punches, kicks, and defensive moves under strict codes of respect and control. 		Kung Fu Sanda

Kung Fu Sanda, or Sanshou, is a Chinese full-contact sport combining kung fu techniques with modern fighting. It includes punches, kicks, and takedowns, enhancing speed and tactical skills.
